What do you want to say about this application?

Your comment can support, object or provide general feedback on the proposed development.

Make sure you include:

  • how the proposed development may affect you and/or your business and community
  • relevant detail to support your views

You should focus on planning-related matters. These can include, but are not limited to, the environment, local economy, transport links, access to services and quality of life factors such as noise, lighting and general living conditions.

If you want to add any supporting documents to your comment, you can do this on the next page.

Your comment will be published on this website along with your name, and the name of your organisation where applicable.

Do not include in your comment any personal information that could identify you or another person.

Guidance on what not to include

Do not include:

  • personal data such as names, addresses, contact details, signatures and photos that could identify you or anyone else
  • links to third party websites, except GOV.UK or chartered professional websites
  • sensitive information

Sensitive information refers to information that:

  • could identify a child
  • relates to health
  • relates to crime 

It also means any information relating to an individual's:

  • race or ethnic origin
  • political or religious beliefs
  • trade union membership
  • genetic or biometric data
  • physical characteristics
  • mental health or sexual health
  • sexual orientation and sex life

If you include any personal data, third party links or sensitive data in your comment or supporting documents, this will be redacted and will not be taken into account by the Planning Inspectors.

If you include any racist, inflammatory or abusive language, your written representation will be rejected.
