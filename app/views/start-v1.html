Use this service to have your say about a Crown Development Application. We need your personal details and your comments about CROWN/2025/0000020.

The application can be inspected under the Documents heading.

If you're under 18, get an adult to submit your comment for you.

Writing your comments

Your comments must be about what you consider to be the main issues and impacts. You should include as much detail as possible and cover anything that may affect your day-to-day life.

Your comments will be:

  • taken into account before a decision is made on the application
  • published on this website along with your name

You must submit your comments before 11:59pm on 12 December 2025.

At the Planning Inspectorate, we value your privacy. This statement outlines how we process your personal data.

When you submit a comment on a non-urgent Crown development application, we’ll ask for your name and email address.

Your name and comment will be published on the Find a Crown development application service. We will only use your email address to communicate with you about your submission and will not use your data for any other, unrelated purpose.

We’ll process all of your data in line with our legal obligation under The Town and Country Planning (Crown Development Applications) (Procedure and Written Representations) Order 2025.

You can read full details of how we process your data in our GOV. UK Customer privacy notice.

You have the right to access and correct your data. To do this, contact the Planning Inspectorate Senior Data Protection Manager at dataprotection@planninginspectorate.gov.uk.
