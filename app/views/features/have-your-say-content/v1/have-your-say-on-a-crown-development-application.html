Have your say on a Crown development application

Use this service to submit a written representation with your views on the proposed development. A written representation includes your comment and any supporting documents.

Your comment and any supporting documents will be:

  • published on this website along with your name (and the name of your organisation if applicable)
  • considered by the Planning Inspectors before a decision is made on the application
! Warning You must submit your comment and any supporting documents by 11:59pm on 12 December 2027.

Using AI in written representations

You can use artificial intelligence (AI) tools to help draft or translate your written representation. You must check that everything is accurate and reflects your own views.

Include a short statement in your representation to say that you used AI.

At the Planning Inspectorate, we value your privacy. This statement outlines how we process your personal data.

When you submit a written representation on a non-urgent Crown development application, we’ll ask for your name and email address.

Your name and written representation will be published on the Find a Crown development application service. We will only use your email address to communicate with you about your submission and will not use your data for any other, unrelated purpose.

We’ll process all of your data in line with our legal obligation under The Town and Country Planning (Crown Development Applications) (Procedure and Written Representations) Order 2025.

You can read full details of how we process your data in our GOV.UK Customer Privacy Notice

You have the right to access and correct your data. To do this, contact the Planning Inspectorate Senior Data Protection Manager at dataprotection@planninginspectorate.gov.uk.
